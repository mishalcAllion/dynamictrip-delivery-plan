Risks & Mitigations

All risks identified as of Apr 9 progress check. Reviewed with engineering team.

HIGH IMPACT MEDIUM LIKELIHOOD
Backend sync not ready by Apr 10 EOD
Backend War Room has 4 devs focused on Mongo-Notion sync. If incomplete by EOD Apr 10, mobile cannot integrate and the demo flow breaks. This is the highest-risk item on the critical path.
Mitigation
Backend War Room format — 4 devs on same task. Fallback: team works through the weekend to deliver the dev build.
MEDIUM IMPACT MEDIUM LIKELIHOOD
Legacy account migration more complex than expected
Legacy accounts lack required fields for the new Notion-backed architecture. Full migration script is not feasible — manual effort needed per account.
Mitigation
Scoped to test users only (Prahar, Sunthar, Nirav). No impact on new account creation. Dummy data for non-essential fields. Legacy mass migration deferred post-demo.
MEDIUM IMPACT MEDIUM LIKELIHOOD
Feedback on Apr 13-14 requires immediate fixes
Sunthar/Prahar preview on Apr 13 may surface critical bugs. Core backend team availability is limited on Apr 13-14. Only critical and high priority fixes can be addressed in this window.
Mitigation
Core team on standby for critical items only. Proposed: Dustin handles low-priority must-have items. Non-critical feedback tracked for post-demo sprint.
LOW IMPACT LOW LIKELIHOOD
Limited availability window — Apr 13-14
Backend team operating at reduced capacity Apr 13-14. Coverage is confirmed for critical items.
Mitigation
Team confirmed availability and on standby.
